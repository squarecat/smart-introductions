ABOUT US

Smart was founded by experienced recruiter Iain Johnson (Ex Shilton Sharpe Quarry and DMJ Legal). We represent opportunities on behalf of agencies, search firms and businesses in London and internationally.

Good recruiters are busy. You do not have time for on-desk calls or poorly constructed Inmails about opportunities not tailored to you. You need trusted experts providing detailed market insight with whom you can build a relationship.

We act as career confidants, helping you make informed decisions so you can make the right move, at the right time. In the interim, we will keep you appraised of market trends, salary information and competitor analysis.

Smart have constructed a client-base to cater for all ambitions. Whether you are motivated to move into industry, abroad, seek greater working flexibility or a more transparent commission structure, we can provide options and opinions.

During processes with us, we will ensure your time is never wasted. We will always provide timely and candid feedback. We only represent clients we would work for ourselves and that we are confident fit the criteria you are looking for.

We are always available outside standard working hours. So whether you are simply curious about billings and commission structures elsewhere, or would like to hear about current opportunities, please book a call with us at a convenient time or register here.
