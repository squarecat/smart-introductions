ABOUT US

Smart identify, evaluate then introduce leading recruitment talent to professional services agencies, search firms and businesses. We specialise in recruiting experienced consultants and talent acquisition professionals across in London and internationally.

Our aim is to deliver true partnership through high level service and post completion accountability. We have invested significantly in our processes and post placement guarantee to provide solutions that are as close to permanent as possible.

We deliver creative recruitment campaigns for clients, working closely to ensure your opportunities are differentiated and represented effectively. All clients are different, from their commission structures to their cultures and working practices. As your brand ambassadors, we pride ourselves on representing your opportunity in a detailed and professional fashion.

Smart was founded in 2015 by experienced recruiter Iain Johnson (Ex Shilton Sharpe Quarry and DMJ Legal). After a career in law, Iain spent 5 successful years recruiting lawyers for clients including Apple, Aviva, Endemol and News UK, providing excellent insight into the world of corporate recruitment. Iain understands the rigours of attracting and retaining staff after 18 months as a hiring manager with DMJ Legal. Iain has an excellent network, having worked with and interviewed a number of the top performers in the industry.
